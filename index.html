Dan Lehner is a trombonist, composer, educator and writer living in New York. He has made a name for himself in the Latin, jazz and brass band circuits and currently leads the original music trio True East. He teaches a private brass studio for musicians of all ages and skill levels and reviews jazz and creative music for multiple online platforms.

Dan Lehner has been an active musician, teacher and composer in the New York area for 7 years. Born in Hamilton, New Jersey, he graduated from the William Paterson Jazz Performance program (studying with trombonists such as John Mosca and Tim Newman). Since moving to New York, he has performed with artists like Orrin Evans, Norah Jones, Pharoahe Monch, PitchBlak BrassBand and can regularly be seen playing with bands like High and Mighty Brass Band, Avenida B, Bombrasstico and many, many more

With the band Karikatura, Lehner has played venues and festivals throughout Germany, The Netherlands, Belgium, Denmark, Sweden, Switzerland and France. In 2017, they were selected as cultural ambassadors as part of the U.S. State Department's "American Music Abroad" program, playing concerts and conducting workshops in Chile, Panama, Haiti, Mexico and the Dominican Republic.
